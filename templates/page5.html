Fitness Plans

Skill Improvement

  1. Speed and Agility
    • Schedule:

      3 days a week of ladder drills and sprint intervals.

    • Goal:

      Improve reaction times and quick direction changes.

  2. Strength Training
    • Schedule:

      2 days a week focusing on core and lower body exercises.

    • Goal:

      Enhance stability and explosive power for jumps or kicks.

Injury Prevention

  1. Warm-Up Routine
    • 10 minutes of dynamic stretches targeting major muscle groups.
    • Example: High knees, arm circles, and lunges.
  2. Cool-Down Routine
    • 10 minutes of static stretches to improve flexibility and reduce soreness.
    • Example: Hamstring stretch, calf stretch, and shoulder rolls.

Mental Preparation

Building Resilience

  1. Visualization Techniques:
    • Spend 5 minutes before practice imagining successful plays or moves.
    • Helps build confidence and reduce pre-game anxiety.
  2. Positive Self-Talk:
    • Replace negative thoughts like “I can’t.” with affirmations like “I can.”
    • Keeps you motivated and focused.

Staying Focused in Competition

  1. Mindfulness Exercises:
    • Practice deep breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
    • Reduces stress and keeps you centered during intense moments.
  2. Pre-Game Routine:
    • Develop a consistent ritual like listening to music, meditating, or reviewing strategies.
    • Signals your brain to get into performance mode.
