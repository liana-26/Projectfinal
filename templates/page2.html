Mission

Our mission is to bring communities together through the joy of sports. We want to create an environment where local sports are accessible to everyone, with a special focus on empowering girls in athletics. By encouraging participation, offering resources, and breaking down the barriers that hold people back, we aim to foster a culture that values active lifestyles, teamwork, and equal opportunities for everyone.

Vision

We dream of a world where sports unite people and allow every individual—no matter their gender, age, or background—to explore their full potential. Through working together, being innovative, and advocating for change, we hope to develop a supportive, inclusive community where sports are a driving force for personal growth, connection among neighbors, and positive change.

The Team

Liana Linson- Founder & Visionary

Liana is an football player with a passion for community development. She created a platform that unites people through the power of athletics. At AthleticUsHub, she oversees strategic direction and partnerships, ensuring the platform remains inclusive and impactful.

Raj Patel- Head of Operations

Raj, a sports event manager, manages the day-to-day operations of AthleticUsHub. From coordinating events to streamlining league registrations, his organizational expertise ensures everything runs smoothly.

Liz Linson - Advocacy Lead for Girls in Sports

A strong advocate for gender equality, Liz spearheads initiatives focused on empowering girls through sports. Her work includes designing mentorship programs, curating training content, and sharing inspirational success stories.

Carlos Mendes - Technical Director

With a background in software engineering and a love for sports, Carlos leads the development of AthleticUsHub user-friendly platform. From the interactive event calendar to the training resource hub, his innovations bring the site to life.

Lila Thompson - Community Engagement Manager

Lila builds and nurtures AthleticUsHub's community by connecting with athletes, coaches, and volunteers. She manages social media outreach, organizes forums, and ensures everyone feels welcome and heard.

Marcus Taylor - Volunteer Coordinator

Marcus works tirelessly to recruit, train, and recognize the volunteers who power AthleticUsHub events and programs. His goal is to ensure every volunteer feels valued and has a meaningful role.
