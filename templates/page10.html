What is AthleticUsLeague?

AthleticUsLeague is a platform designed to promote community sports, provide resources for athletes, and advocate for inclusivity, especially for girls in sports.

Who can participate in AthleticUsLeague activities?

Our events and programs are open to athletes of all ages and skill levels. Specific eligibility criteria are outlined for each event or initiative.

How can I get involved with AthleticUsLeague?

You can join as a participant in our events, volunteer for event management, or sign up for coaching and mentorship opportunities.

What sports does AthleticUsLeague focus on?

We cater to a variety of sports, including soccer, basketball, athletics, tennis, and more.

What types of events does AthleticUsLeague organize?

We organize a wide range of events, including races, obstacle courses, team sports tournaments, and training workshops.

How do I register for an event?

Registration, for now, can only be completed in person. Simply come to any of our offices, fill out the registration form, and submit any required documents or fees.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can sign up for as many events as you are eligible for, provided they don’t overlap in timing.

What should I bring to an event?

Participants should bring appropriate gear (e.g., sportswear, safety equipment), hydration, and any required documents such as medical clearance as well as other things mentioned.

What is the Girls in Sports Initiative?

This initiative aims to encourage and support girls in sports through mentorship, training programs, and community networking opportunities.

How can girls benefit from your programs? And can boys join the initiatives?

Girls gain access to skill-building sessions, leadership workshops, career mentorship, and platforms to connect with peers and role models.
While some initiatives are specifically designed for girls, many of our programs and events are open to everyone.

Are there scholarships available for young athletes?

Yes, AthleticUsLeague partners with organizations to provide information and resources about sports scholarships. More information on this will be uploaded on the site soon.

Do volunteers need prior experience?

While prior experience is helpful, we welcome passionate individuals and provide training for most roles.
